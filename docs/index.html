Problem statement

London travel gets harder when signal drops and directions get confusing.

Visitors often switch between map apps, translation tools, and station signs while trying to understand the network. Underground, that experience breaks down fast.

NAV AiDE focuses on the MVP need: helping tourists find their way across London transport with offline guidance, multilingual support, and simple next-step answers.

Solution overview

One lightweight guide for navigating London from start to finish.

NAV AiDE combines offline maps, local routing data, and AI-assisted guidance designed for London visitors. It is built to support practical travel questions without depending on a constant mobile connection.

The experience stays focused on the MVP: route help, map context, multilingual travel support, and a fallback for moments when the user feels lost.
